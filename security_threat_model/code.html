Secret Hygiene

GROUP-CHAT LEAK PROTECTION ACTIVE

The bot watches for fund-moving commands (which carry an OTP) typed outside a DM. When one shows up in a group, it deletes the message if it has permission and burns that OTP immediately. No account freeze, no key rotation, just an immediate scrub so the leaked code can't be replayed.

What we are not claiming

IMMUTABILITY

The bot is software. Software has bugs. We do not claim 100% bug-free operation.

TELEGRAM SECURITY

We cannot secure your Telegram account. If your session is hijacked, your fund commands can be sent.

SOLANA UPTIME

We cannot bypass chain-level congestion or RPC failures. Orders may fail during peak volatility.

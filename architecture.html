◇ Data flow · the custody boundary

How it's wired.

A message enters at Telegram and only ever reaches the signer through an auth gate. The key sits on the far side of a boundary the chat layer cannot cross.

01 / MODULES

The pieces.

src/bot

Telegram layer

Receives commands, routes them, suggests the closest command on a typo. Holds no keys and makes no signing decisions on its own.

src/auth

The gate

A numeric-ID (and username) allowlist. Every message passes through here first; grants and denials both hit the audit log. Argon2 password login and OTP replay tracking are planned, not in this build.

src/watch · src/swap

Watchers & swap

SOL and token watchers spot incoming deposits across child accounts and announce them; the swap module routes non-SOL to SOL through Jupiter — Raydium as fallback — with slippage and min-output gates, then closes the emptied account. src/swap/forwardSol drains SOL above the buffer to a cold wallet.

src/split

Splitter planned

Designed to parse /send into amounts + destinations, resolve aliases against a fixed contacts file or a literal address, and build one atomic multi-recipient transaction. Not wired to the bot yet.

src/vault

Signer boundary

One interface, swappable backend: local AES-256-GCM keystore, KMS, or custody. The rest of the app asks it to sign — it never sees the raw key.

src/audit

Ledger

Append-only, hash-chained log of every attempt. verifyAuditChain() re-walks the chain to prove nothing was edited.

02 / A SWEEP, STEP BY STEP

One token's path.

1 · Detect — the token watcher spots an incoming SPL token across the wallet's child accounts and announces it in the group.src/watch/tokenWatcher
2 · Route — if sweep is ON, quote a route to SOL via Jupiter, falling back to Raydium; reject anything that fails the slippage or min-output gate.src/swap/sweep
3 · Sign — the built swap crosses the custody boundary to the signer. The key never comes back out.src/vault/signer
4 · Reclaim + forward — the emptied token account is closed for its rent, and SOL above the hot-wallet buffer forwards to the cold address.src/swap/forwardSol
5 · Log — the attempt and result append to the hash-chained ledger.src/audit/log

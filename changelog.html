Changelog.

Three phases shipped and hardened for 24/7. Everything below runs on devnet; the splitter and TOTP are planned, and mainnet is gated on a custody signer.

planned · not shippedSplitter & auth

Atomic splitter, login & TOTP

Designed, not yet in the running bot: /send paying multiple recipients in one atomic transaction against a contacts file, /login with an Argon2 password, a burn-on-use TOTP for fund-moving commands, and group-chat secret scrubbing.

phase 03 · shippedHardening

Cold-forward, rent reclaim & 24/7 runtime

Swept SOL above a hot-wallet buffer forwards to a cold WITHDRAW_ADDRESS; emptied token accounts are closed and their rent reclaimed. Websocket watchers with a polling backup, a heartbeat, and graceful shutdown keep it running unattended. The config loader hard-refuses mainnet-beta with a local key.

phase 03 · shippedAudit ledger

Hash-chained audit log

Every command attempt — allowed or denied — appends to data/audit.jsonl, each entry's hash folding in the previous one. verifyAuditChain() re-walks the chain to detect any edit.

phase 02 · shippedAuto-sweep

Background auto-sweep with best-rate routing

SOL and token watchers detect incoming deposits across child accounts and announce them; non-SOL tokens swap to SOL through Jupiter — Raydium as fallback — automatically when /autosweep is ON, under slippage and min-output gates. No command needed.

phase 01 · shippedFoundation

Auth & key vault

Numeric-ID (and username) allowlist as the hard access gate, and the swappable Signer interface with a local AES-256-GCM encrypted keystore. Commands: /start, /whoami, /help, /autosweep.
